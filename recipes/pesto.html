Pesto

Description

The prep time is 15 minutes, while the cook time is 15 minutes, which totalled to be 30 minutes.The recipe can serve up to 10 people.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh basil leaves, washed and dried
  • 8 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste
  • ⅓ cup pine nuts, or to taste (Optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine basil leaves, 3 tablespoons olive oil, and garlic in the bowl of a food processor; pulse until basil is chopped. Add Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, salt, and pepper; pulse to combine. Pour in remaining olive oil slowly, with the processor running, until pesto has reached the desired consistency.
